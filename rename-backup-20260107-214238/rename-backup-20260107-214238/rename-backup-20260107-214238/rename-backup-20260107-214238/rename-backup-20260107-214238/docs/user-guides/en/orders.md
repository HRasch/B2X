# Managing Orders

Track and manage your orders at B2X.

---

## 📦 Order Overview

### View Orders

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"My Orders"** or the user icon
3. All orders are displayed chronologically

### Order Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟡 **Received** | Order was received |
| 🔵 **Processing** | Being picked |
| 📦 **Shipped** | On the way to you |
| ✅ **Delivered** | Successfully delivered |
| ❌ **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled |
| 🔄 **Return** | Return in progress |

## 📬 Shipment Tracking

### Tracking Information

After shipping, you will receive:

1. **Email with tracking link**
2. **Tracking number** in order details

### Track Shipment

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Track Shipment"**
3. You will be redirected to the carrier's tracking page

### Carriers

| Carrier | Tracking Page |
|---------|---------------|

## 📄 Download Invoice

### Get Invoice

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Download Invoice"** (PDF)

### Invoice Contents

Your invoice includes:
- Invoice number
- Order number
- Billing address
- Delivery address
- Product list with prices
- VAT breakdown
- Payment information

> ℹ️ B2B customers with valid VAT-ID receive invoices without VAT (reverse-charge)

## 🔄 Reorder

### Place Repeat Order

1. Open a previous order
2. Click **"Reorder"**
3. All products are added to cart
4. Adjust quantities (if desired)
5. Proceed to checkout

> ⚠️ Prices may have changed since the original order

## ❌ Cancel Order

### When is Cancellation Possible?

- ✅ Status "Received" → Cancellation possible
- ✅ Status "Processing" → Contact support
- ❌ Status "Shipped" → Cancellation no longer possible (use return)

### Cancel Order

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Cancel Order"**
3. Select a cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation

### Refund

After cancellation:
- **Credit Card**: Refund within 3-5 business days
- **PayPal**: Immediate refund
- **SEPA Direct Debit**: Refund within 5-7 business days
- **Invoice (B2B)**: Invoice is cancelled

---

## 📦 Returns

### Return Policy

- **Private Customers (B2C)**: 14-day right of withdrawal
- **Business Customers (B2B)**: By agreement

### Request Return

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Request Return"**
3. Select products to return
4. Provide a reason
5. Print the return label

### Process Return

1. Pack the products securely
2. Attach the return label
3. Drop off the package at the carrier

### Refund

After receipt and inspection of the return:
- Refund within 5-7 business days
- You will receive a confirmation email

## 📊 Export Order History

### Export for B2B Customers

1. Go to **"My Orders"**
2. Click **"Export"**
3. Select format:
   - CSV (for Excel)
   - PDF (for accounting)
4. Select time period
5. Click **"Download"**

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where do I find my order number?**  
A: In the order confirmation email and in your account under "My Orders".

**Q: My shipment hasn't arrived, what should I do?**  
A: First check the shipment tracking. Contact support if problems persist.

**Q: Can I change the delivery address after ordering?**  
A: Only with status "Received". Contact support as soon as possible.

**Q: When will I receive my invoice?**  
A: The invoice is sent by email with the shipping confirmation.

**Q: How long are orders stored?**  
A: Your order history is available indefinitely in your account.
