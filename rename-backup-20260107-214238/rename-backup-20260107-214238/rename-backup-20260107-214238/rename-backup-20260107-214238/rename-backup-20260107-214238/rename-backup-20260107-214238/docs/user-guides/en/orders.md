## ‚ùå Cancel Order

### When is Cancellation Possible?

- ‚úÖ Status "Received" ‚Üí Cancellation possible
- ‚úÖ Status "Processing" ‚Üí Contact support
- ‚ùå Status "Shipped" ‚Üí Cancellation no longer possible (use return)

### Cancel Order

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Cancel Order"**
3. Select a cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation

### Refund

After cancellation:
- **Credit Card**: Refund within 3-5 business days
- **PayPal**: Immediate refund
- **SEPA Direct Debit**: Refund within 5-7 business days
- **Invoice (B2B)**: Invoice is cancelled

---

## üì¶ Returns

### Return Policy

- **Private Customers (B2C)**: 14-day right of withdrawal
- **Business Customers (B2B)**: By agreement

### Request Return

1. Open order details
2. Click **"Request Return"**
3. Select products to return
4. Provide a reason
5. Print the return label

### Process Return

1. Pack the products securely
2. Attach the return label
3. Drop off the package at the carrier

### Refund

After receipt and inspection of the return:
- Refund within 5-7 business days
- You will receive a confirmation email

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Where do I find my order number?**  
A: In the order confirmation email and in your account under "My Orders".

**Q: My shipment hasn't arrived, what should I do?**  
A: First check the shipment tracking. Contact support if problems persist.

**Q: Can I change the delivery address after ordering?**  
A: Only with status "Received". Contact support as soon as possible.

**Q: When will I receive my invoice?**  
A: The invoice is sent by email with the shipping confirmation.

**Q: How long are orders stored?**  
A: Your order history is available indefinitely in your account.
