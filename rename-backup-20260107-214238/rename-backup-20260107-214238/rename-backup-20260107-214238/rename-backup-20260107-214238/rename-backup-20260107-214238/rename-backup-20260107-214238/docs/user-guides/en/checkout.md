## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I still change my order?**  
A: Yes, until you click "Place Order" you can change everything.

**Q: When will my credit card be charged?**  
A: Immediately after order completion.

**Q: Will I receive an invoice?**  
A: Yes, the invoice is sent by email and is available in your account.

**Q: What does Reverse-Charge mean?**  
A: For B2B deliveries to other EU countries, no VAT is charged. You declare the VAT yourself.

**Q: My payment was declined, what now?**  
A: Check your card details or choose a different payment method. Contact support if problems persist.
