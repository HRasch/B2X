# Registration at B2X

Create your B2X account in just a few steps.

---

## 🎯 Account Types

B2X offers two account types:

| Type | For whom? | Benefits |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Private Customer (B2C)** | Individuals | Simple registration, gross prices |
| **Business Customer (B2B)** | Companies, businesses | Net prices, reverse-charge, volume discounts |

---

## 👤 Private Customer Registration

### Step 1: Start Registration

1. Click **"Register"** (top right)
2. Select **"Private Customer"**

### Step 2: Personal Details

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Email | Your email address | ✅ Yes |
| Password | Min. 8 characters, upper/lowercase, number | ✅ Yes |
| Confirm Password | Repeat password | ✅ Yes |
| First Name | Your first name | ✅ Yes |
| Last Name | Your last name | ✅ Yes |

### Step 3: Address (Optional)

You can add your address later or enter it now:

- Street & Number
- Postal Code
- City
- Country

### Step 4: Accept Terms

- [ ] I accept the [Terms & Conditions](../terms.md)
- [ ] I have read the [Privacy Policy](../privacy.md)

### Step 5: Create Account

Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 6: Confirm Email

1. Check your inbox
2. Open the email from B2X
3. Click **"Confirm Email"**

> ✅ Your account is now active!

---

## 🏢 Business Customer Registration (B2B)

### Step 1: Start Registration

1. Click **"Register"** (top right)
2. Select **"Business Customer"**

### Step 2: Company Details

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Company Name | Official company name | ✅ Yes |
| VAT-ID | VAT Identification Number | ✅ Yes (EU) |
| Registration Number | Commercial register number | ❌ No |
| Industry | Your industry | ❌ No |

### Step 3: Contact Person

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| First Name | Contact person first name | ✅ Yes |
| Last Name | Contact person last name | ✅ Yes |
| Position | Your position in the company | ❌ No |
| Email | Business email | ✅ Yes |
| Phone | Business phone number | ❌ No |

### Step 4: Business Address

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Street & Number | Company address | ✅ Yes |
| Postal Code | ZIP code | ✅ Yes |
| City | City | ✅ Yes |
| Country | Country | ✅ Yes |

### Step 5: VAT-ID Validation

Your VAT-ID is automatically verified:

- ✅ **Valid**: Reverse-charge is activated
- ❌ **Invalid**: Please check the number

> ℹ️ Validation is performed via the EU VIES system

### Step 6: Set Password

| Requirement | |
|-------------|---|
| Minimum length | 8 characters |
| Uppercase letters | At least 1 |
| Lowercase letters | At least 1 |
| Number | At least 1 |
| Special character | Recommended |

### Step 7: Accept Terms

- [ ] I accept the [Terms & Conditions](../terms.md)
- [ ] I accept the [B2B Terms](../b2b-terms.md)
- [ ] I have read the [Privacy Policy](../privacy.md)

### Step 8: Create Account

Click **"Create Business Account"**

### Step 9: Verification

B2B accounts are manually reviewed:

1. You will receive a confirmation email
2. Our team reviews your details (1-2 business days)
3. You will receive an email after activation

> ⏳ During review, you can already use the shop (B2C prices)

## 🔐 Password Requirements

A secure password contains:

✅ At least 8 characters  
✅ Upper and lowercase letters  
✅ At least one number  
✅ Special characters (recommended)  
❌ No name or date of birth  
❌ Not the same as other services  

**Example of a strong password:**
```
Buy!B2C_2026
```

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I switch to B2B later?**  
A: Yes, contact support with your company details.

**Q: How long does B2B activation take?**  
A: Usually 1-2 business days.

**Q: What is the VAT-ID?**  
A: The VAT Identification Number for EU companies (e.g., DE123456789).

**Q: Can I have multiple users for a company account?**  
A: Yes, after activation you can invite additional users.

**Q: My email confirmation didn't arrive**  
A: Check your spam folder. Request a new email after 5 minutes.
