# Communication and Training Plans

## Overview
This document outlines plans for communicating product strategy and providing training to ensure all teams understand and contribute to strategic goals.

## Communication Channels

### Strategic Communication Cadence
- **Weekly**: Team standups include strategy updates (5 minutes)
- **Bi-weekly**: Cross-team strategy alignment meetings
- **Monthly**: All-hands strategy reviews with Q&A
- **Quarterly**: Strategy deep-dive sessions and workshops
- **Annually**: Strategy planning and vision sessions

### Communication Tools
- **Strategy Dashboard**: Real-time visibility into strategic goals and progress
- **Strategy Newsletter**: Monthly updates on strategic initiatives
- **Strategy Wiki**: Centralized knowledge base for strategy documentation
- **Strategy Slack Channel**: Daily discussions and updates
- **Strategy Office Hours**: Open sessions for strategy questions

## Training Programs

### Strategy Foundations Training
**Target**: All team members
**Duration**: 4 hours
**Content**:
- Product vision and mission
- Strategic goals and objectives
- Decision-making frameworks
- Strategy contribution guidelines

**Format**: Interactive workshop with case studies
**Frequency**: Quarterly refreshers

### Role-Specific Strategy Training
**@ProductOwner**: Advanced prioritization and roadmap planning
**@Architect**: Strategy-driven architectural design
**@ScrumMaster**: Strategic sprint planning and execution
**@TechLead**: Technical decision-making with strategic context
**@FinOps**: Financial strategy and ROI analysis
**@Legal**: Strategic risk and compliance management
**@SARAH**: Cross-agent coordination and governance

### Leadership Strategy Training
**Target**: Team leads and managers
**Duration**: 8 hours
**Content**:
- Strategy cascading and communication
- Strategic decision-making
- Team alignment and motivation
- Strategy execution monitoring

## Communication Plans by Agent

### @ProductOwner Communication Plan
- Owns strategy narrative and messaging
- Leads quarterly strategy communication campaigns
- Develops strategy visualization and storytelling
- Coordinates with marketing for external strategy communication

### @Architect Communication Plan
- Communicates architectural strategy in technical terms
- Develops architecture strategy roadmaps
- Creates technical strategy documentation
- Leads technical strategy workshops

### @ScrumMaster Communication Plan
- Integrates strategy into agile ceremonies
- Develops team-level strategy communication rituals
- Creates sprint goals tied to strategic objectives
- Facilitates strategy-focused retrospectives

### @TechLead Communication Plan
- Communicates technical strategy implications
- Develops code-level strategy guidelines
- Creates technical decision frameworks
- Leads technical strategy mentoring

### @FinOps Communication Plan
- Communicates financial strategy and constraints
- Develops budget strategy communication
- Creates financial strategy dashboards
- Leads cost optimization strategy discussions

### @Legal Communication Plan
- Communicates compliance strategy requirements
- Develops risk strategy communication
- Creates legal strategy guidelines
- Leads regulatory strategy workshops

### @SARAH Communication Plan
- Coordinates unified strategy communication
- Maintains strategy communication calendar
- Develops cross-agent communication protocols
- Monitors communication effectiveness

## Training Delivery Methods
- **Live Workshops**: Interactive sessions with Q&A
- **Self-Paced Online Courses**: Video modules with quizzes
- **Mentoring Programs**: One-on-one strategy guidance
- **Lunch & Learn Sessions**: Informal strategy discussions
- **Certification Programs**: Strategy competency validation

## Measurement & Feedback
- **Communication Effectiveness**: Surveys and feedback forms
- **Training Completion**: Tracking and certification rates
- **Strategy Understanding**: Knowledge assessments
- **Behavior Change**: Observation of strategy-aligned decisions

## Implementation Timeline
- **Month 1**: Launch communication channels and initial training
- **Month 2**: Roll out role-specific training programs
- **Month 3**: Establish feedback mechanisms and measurement
- **Month 6**: Full implementation with continuous improvement

## Resources Required
- Training budget allocation
- Communication tool licenses
- Training content development team
- Strategy communication coordinator role
