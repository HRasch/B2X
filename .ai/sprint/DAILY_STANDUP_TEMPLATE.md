## ðŸš¨ Blocker Escalation

**If blockers appear:**

1. **Name it clearly**
   - "Waiting for X from Y"
   - "Technical issue preventing Z"
   - "Decision needed on A"

2. **Log impact**
   - "Blocks Issue #57 completion"
   - "Delays Phase 2 start"

3. **Propose solution**
   - "Need decision by EOD"
   - "Escalate to @TechLead"
   - "Create emergency task"

4. **Update tracking**
   - Add to ITERATION_001_TRACKING.md
   - Mark issue as blocked
   - Notify owner
