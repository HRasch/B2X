# End User FAQs - Account Management

## How do I create a B2Connect account?
**Answer:** To create a B2Connect account, visit the B2Connect Store website and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You'll need to provide:
- A valid email address
- A secure password (minimum 8 characters with numbers and symbols)
- Your basic profile information

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to activate your account. Once activated, you can start shopping immediately.

## How do I reset my password?
**Answer:** If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps:

1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address associated with your account
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new secure password
5. Log in with your new password

The reset link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

## How do I update my profile information?
**Answer:** To update your profile information:

1. Log in to your B2Connect account
2. Click on your name/avatar in the top right corner
3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
4. Update your information in the respective fields:
   - Personal details (name, phone, address)
   - Preferences (language, notifications)
   - Security settings (password, two-factor authentication)
5. Click "Save Changes"

All changes are saved immediately. For security-sensitive changes, you may need to verify your identity.
