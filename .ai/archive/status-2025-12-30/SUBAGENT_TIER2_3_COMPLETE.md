## Lessons Learned & Insights

### What Worked Well
1. **Consistent format** across all agents (easy to onboard)
2. **Per-task context model** (reduces bloat, improves focus)
3. **Explicit delegation pattern** (clear, auditable, trackable)
4. **Gradual rollout** (3 phases allows validation gates)
5. **Specialized expertise** (SubAgents outperform generalists)

### Areas for Improvement
1. **Agent interdependencies** (document cross-references)
2. **Conflict resolution** (add examples for overlapping domains)
3. **Feedback loop** (structured metrics from teams)
4. **Documentation** (keep guides updated as agents evolve)
5. **Context optimization** (retire unused agents, consolidate)

### Recommendations for Phase 4+
1. **Tier 4 SubAgents** (domain-specific e.g., @SubAgent-CatalogDDD)
2. **SubAgent-to-SubAgent delegation** (complex tasks)
3. **Learning from usage** (improve instructions based on outputs)
4. **Performance monitoring** (track per-agent efficiency)
5. **Community contributions** (team-created agents)
