## FAQ Reference Document

```markdown
# Phase 1 SubAgent FAQ

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I use a SubAgent?**
A: Write a clear request like "@SubAgent-APIDesign, design REST pattern for bulk import"
   Delegate NOW (don't schedule). Output appears in .ai/issues/{your-task-id}/ in 2-5 min.

**Q: Which agent should I use?**
A: Check your team's agents (sent in email). Use the one matching your need.
   If unsure, post in #subagent-support.

**Q: Is there a wrong way to delegate?**
A: Vague requests get vague outputs. Be specific about what you're designing/building.

## Delegation & Output

**Q: How long does a delegation take?**
A: Usually 2-5 minutes from submission to output.

**Q: Where do outputs appear?**
A: `.ai/issues/{your-task-id}/` - check there after delegating.

**Q: Can I request clarifications?**
A: Yes - post "Request clarification on [item]" and agent will refine.

**Q: Can multiple teams delegate at the same time?**
A: Yes - all delegations execute in parallel. No queuing.

## Quality & Satisfaction

**Q: What if the output isn't what I expected?**
A: That's feedback we want. Post in #subagent-support with what didn't match.

**Q: Am I required to use the output?**
A: No - SubAgents provide guidance. Your team decides what's useful.
   Adapt, customize, or skip if not helpful.

**Q: What if the output is incorrect?**
A: Report it in #subagent-support with details. @TechLead escalates to @SARAH.

## Phase 1 Specifics

**Q: How long is Phase 1?**
A: One week (Jan 6-10). Then Friday phase gate decision on Phase 2.

**Q: What makes Phase 1 successful?**
A: >50% of teams actively delegating + >4.0/5.0 satisfaction + >60% context reduction.

**Q: What happens if Phase 1 isn't successful?**
A: Extra training Tue-Wed (Jan 12-13), Phase 1 continues with improvements.

**Q: When does Phase 2 start?**
A: Jan 13 (if Phase 1 >50% adoption), or later if more training needed.

## Support & Escalation

**Q: What if I have a blocker?**
A: Post in #subagent-support with [BLOCKER] tag. @TechLead will escalate immediately.

**Q: How fast is support?**
A: <15 minutes during business hours.

**Q: What if it's after hours?**
A: Critical blockers only. Non-urgent will be handled next business day.

**Q: How do I suggest improvements?**
A: Post in #subagent-support. Constructive feedback shapes Phase 2 improvements.

## Metrics & Tracking

**Q: Why are we tracking metrics?**
A: To understand what's working and what needs improvement for Phase 2.

**Q: How will adoption be measured?**
A: # of tasks using delegation / total tasks per team.

**Q: What happens to my feedback?**
A: It's logged, analyzed weekly, and feeds into agent improvements (learning cycle).
```
