# B2Connect Business Requirements

## Executive Sponsorship

**Version**: 1.0  
**Date**: December 25, 2024  
**Status**: Active  

## 1. Business Vision

B2Connect is an integrated B2B/B2C e-commerce and procurement platform designed to:

1. **Enable Multi-Channel Retail**: Support simultaneous B2B and B2C sales channels
2. **Streamline Procurement**: Connect to enterprise procurement platforms for automated order management
3. **Optimize Supply Chain**: Provide real-time visibility across inventory and suppliers
4. **Drive Revenue Growth**: Enable new sales channels and business models
5. **Reduce Operational Costs**: Automate manual processes and improve efficiency

## 2. Business Objectives

### Revenue Objectives
- **New Revenue Streams**: Enable B2B wholesale channel in addition to B2C retail
- **Market Expansion**: Support international markets with multi-currency, multi-language
- **Marketplace Integration**: Enable sales through Amazon, eBay, and other platforms
- **Subscription Services**: Offer recurring revenue through subscriptions and memberships

### Operational Objectives
- **Cost Reduction**: Reduce order processing time from hours to minutes
- **Inventory Optimization**: Improve inventory turnover and reduce carrying costs
- **Process Automation**: Automate 80% of routine procurement tasks
- **Supplier Management**: Consolidate and optimize supplier relationships
- **Quality Improvement**: Reduce order errors and returns through better systems

### Strategic Objectives
- **Digital Transformation**: Modernize technology stack and capabilities
- **Scalability**: Support 10x growth without infrastructure changes
- **Data Intelligence**: Enable data-driven decision making
- **Customer Experience**: Provide industry-leading digital experience
- **Competitive Positioning**: Differentiate through technology and capabilities

## 3. Target Market Segments

### Primary Segments
1. **B2C Retailers**: Companies selling consumer products online
2. **B2B Suppliers**: Companies selling to other businesses
3. **Multi-channel Retailers**: Companies selling across multiple channels
4. **Enterprise Procurement**: Large companies with complex procurement needs

### Customer Profiles

#### B2C Retailer
- Revenue: $10M - $500M
- Products: Electronics, fashion, home goods, etc.
- Current: Separate B2B/B2C systems or manual processes
- Pain Points: Inventory fragmentation, limited B2B capabilities, manual B2B orders

#### B2B Supplier
- Revenue: $50M - $1B
- Products: Industrial, components, materials, etc.
- Current: Legacy order systems, manual procurement integration
- Pain Points: Manual order entry, inventory visibility, supplier coordination

#### Enterprise
- Revenue: $1B+
- Complexity: Multi-division, multiple suppliers, complex approval workflows
- Current: ERP + Procurement platform + Manual integrations
- Pain Points: System integration, data consistency, process automation

## 8. Risk Assessment

### Technical Risks
- **Risk**: Performance under load
- **Mitigation**: Load testing, infrastructure planning, caching strategy
- **Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium

- **Risk**: Procurement platform integration complexity
- **Mitigation**: Early engagement with platform partners, extensive testing
- **Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium

- **Risk**: Data migration from legacy systems
- **Mitigation**: Phased migration, parallel running, comprehensive testing
- **Impact**: Medium | **Likelihood**: Medium

### Business Risks
- **Risk**: Lower than expected adoption
- **Mitigation**: Clear value proposition, user training, early adopter program
- **Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Low

- **Risk**: Competitive pressure from existing players
- **Mitigation**: Differentiation through integration, superior UX
- **Impact**: High | **Likelihood**: Medium

- **Risk**: Market changes in B2B procurement
- **Mitigation**: Flexibility of architecture, regular feature updates
- **Impact**: Medium | **Likelihood**: Medium
