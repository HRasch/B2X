### Browse the Catalog

1. **Browse Categories**
   - Click on a category in the navigation
   - Subcategories are displayed

2. **Product List**
   - Products are displayed as cards
   - Each card shows: Image, Name, Price, Availability

### Using Search

1. **Search Field**
   - Enter a search term (e.g., product name, SKU)
   - Press Enter or click the magnifying glass

2. **Search Results**
   - Relevant products are displayed
   - Sort by: Relevance, Price, Name

3. **Apply Filters**
   - Select category
   - Enter price range
   - Select brand

## â“ FAQ

**Q: Can I shop without an account?**  
A: Yes for browsing, but you need an account to place orders.

**Q: How long do items stay in the cart?**  
A: Your cart is saved for 30 days (when logged in).

**Q: Can I save my cart?**  
A: Yes, log in and your cart is automatically saved.

**Q: Why do I see different prices?**  
A: B2B customers see net prices, B2C customers see gross prices incl. VAT.
