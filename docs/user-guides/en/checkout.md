# Checkout Process

This guide walks you through the 3-step checkout process at B2X.

---

## 📋 Overview

Checkout consists of three steps:

```
[1. Address] → [2. Shipping] → [3. Review & Payment]
```

---

## Step 1: Shipping Address

### Enter Address

Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| First Name | Your first name | ✅ Yes |
| Last Name | Your last name | ✅ Yes |
| Street & Number | Delivery address | ✅ Yes |
| Postal Code | ZIP code | ✅ Yes |
| City | City | ✅ Yes |
| Country | Delivery country | ✅ Yes |

### Use Saved Address

If you're logged in:
1. Select a saved address
2. Or click **"New Address"**

### Continue

Click **"Continue to Shipping"**

---

## Step 2: Select Shipping Method

### Available Shipping Methods

Different options are shown depending on delivery country:

| Shipping Method | Delivery Time | Price |
|-----------------|---------------|-------|
| **Standard Shipping** | 3-5 business days | from €4.95 |
| **Express Shipping** | 1-2 business days | from €9.95 |
| **Pickup** | Immediately | Free |

> ℹ️ Shipping costs vary by weight and destination

### Make Selection

1. Click on the desired shipping method
2. The selection is highlighted in blue
3. The total price is updated

### Continue

Click **"Continue to Review"**

---

## Step 3: Review & Payment

### Review Order

Check all details:

- ✅ **Delivery address** correct?
- ✅ **Products** and quantities correct?
- ✅ **Shipping method** selected?
- ✅ **Total price** understood?

### Price Summary

```
Subtotal:             €  99.00
+ Shipping:           €   4.95
─────────────────────────────
Net total:            € 103.95
+ VAT (19%):          €  19.75
─────────────────────────────
TOTAL:                € 123.70
```

### Select Payment Method

Available payment methods:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 💳 **Credit Card** | Visa, Mastercard, American Express |
| 🏦 **SEPA Direct Debit** | Bank account debit |
| 💰 **PayPal** | Redirect to PayPal |
| 📄 **Invoice** | B2B customers only |

### Accept Terms

- [ ] I have read and accept the [Terms & Conditions](../terms.md)
- [ ] I have read the [Privacy Policy](../privacy.md)

### Complete Order

Click **"Place Order"**

---

## ✅ Order Confirmation

After successful order:

1. **Confirmation page** is displayed
   - Order number
   - Summary

2. **Email confirmation**
   - Sent to your email address
   - Contains all order details
   - Link to tracking

---

## 🏢 For B2B Customers

### VAT-ID Validation

If you have a valid EU VAT-ID:

1. Enter your VAT-ID
2. Click **"Validate"**
3. On successful validation: **Reverse-Charge** is applied

> ✅ Intra-community delivery without VAT

### Billing Address

B2B customers can specify a separate billing address:
- Click **"Different Billing Address"**
- Fill in the fields

---

## 🔙 Navigate Back

You can return to a previous step at any time:

- Click **"Back"**
- Or click on a step in the progress indicator

> ⚠️ Your entries are preserved

---

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I still change my order?**  
A: Yes, until you click "Place Order" you can change everything.

**Q: When will my credit card be charged?**  
A: Immediately after order completion.

**Q: Will I receive an invoice?**  
A: Yes, the invoice is sent by email and is available in your account.

**Q: What does Reverse-Charge mean?**  
A: For B2B deliveries to other EU countries, no VAT is charged. You declare the VAT yourself.

**Q: My payment was declined, what now?**  
A: Check your card details or choose a different payment method. Contact support if problems persist.
